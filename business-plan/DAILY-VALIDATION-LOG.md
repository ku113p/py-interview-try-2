## Decision Thresholds

| Metric | Green | Yellow | Red |
|--------|-------|--------|-----|
| Visitors | 200+ | 50-199 | <50 |
| Signups | 30+ | 10-29 | <10 |
| Conv Rate | 15%+ | 5-14% | <5% |
| High-Intent | 5+ | 2-4 | <2 |

**Green** = All or most metrics hit targets → BUILD IT
**Yellow** = Mixed results → ITERATE and retry
**Red** = Low across board → PIVOT or validate manually
